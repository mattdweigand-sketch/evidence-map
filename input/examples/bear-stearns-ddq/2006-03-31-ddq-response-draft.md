# DDQ response under audit: Bear Stearns High-Grade Structured Credit Strategies Fund

Status: draft. Data as of 2006-03-31.

Each statement below is quoted or restated verbatim from the completed AIMA due diligence questionnaire for the Bear Stearns High-Grade Structured Credit Strategies Fund (`aima-ddq-bear-stearns-high-grade-fund.pdf`), with FCIC Bates page cites. OCR spelling of "Bear Steams" is normalized to "Bear Stearns."

## Manager and ownership

As of March 31, 2006, BSAM managed a total of $36.1 billion of assets, including $15.7 billion in equity products, $15.0 billion in fixed income products and $5.4 billion in alternative investments. [DDQ p.5, BSAMFCIC 00000302]

The alternative investments total includes the $1.5 billion in assets managed by the HGSCS team as of March 31, 2006. [DDQ p.5, BSAMFCIC 00000302]

Bear Stearns employees hold approximately 40% of Bear Stearns' stock with the remainder publicly held. [DDQ p.5, BSAMFCIC 00000302]

Private Capital Management, L.P. owns 6.1% of the common stock per a February 2006 SEC filing. [DDQ p.5, BSAMFCIC 00000302]

James Cayne, Chief Executive Officer of Bear Stearns, holds 5.6% of the firm's stock. [DDQ p.5, BSAMFCIC 00000302]

## Fund structure and inception

The onshore and offshore feeders both commenced operations on October 1, 2003, investing through the Bear Stearns High-Grade Structured Credit Strategies Master Fund Ltd. in a master-feeder arrangement. [DDQ p.7, BSAMFCIC 00000304]

In addition to the onshore and offshore feeders, 2x and 3x leveraged versions of the Fund are available for investment. [DDQ p.7, BSAMFCIC 00000304]

The Fund's advisor is registered with the SEC. [DDQ p.7, BSAMFCIC 00000304]

## Fees and redemption terms

The management fee is 2% of the net asset value. [DDQ p.8, BSAMFCIC 00000305]

The Fund charges a 20% incentive fee that is subject to a lifetime high watermark. [DDQ p.8, BSAMFCIC 00000305]

A shareholder may withdraw at the annual anniversary upon not less than 60 days prior written notice without a withdrawal fee, and may make special withdrawals as of the last business day of any month upon not less than 40 days prior written notice, subject to a 2% withdrawal fee. [DDQ p.8, BSAMFCIC 00000305]

## Fund assets

The fund's net assets were $1.495 billion as of March 31, 2006, up from $1.267 billion as of March 31, 2005. [DDQ p.10, BSAMFCIC 00000307]

The largest investor represents about 2% of the Master Fund's assets. [DDQ p.10, BSAMFCIC 00000307]

The Onshore Feeder has approximately $450 million, the Offshore Feeder has approximately $1.0 billion, and a Yen Feeder has approximately $30 million. [DDQ p.10, BSAMFCIC 00000307]

## Service providers

The auditor is Deloitte & Touche LLP. [DDQ p.10, BSAMFCIC 00000307]

BSAM has had a relationship with Deloitte & Touche since the beginning of its hedge fund business in 1995. [DDQ p.11, BSAMFCIC 00000308]

## Key personnel
